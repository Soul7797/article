Godfrey: The Latest Tesla FSD Update

The new Godfrey update pushes the boundaries of autonomous driving. It harnesses full-resolution AI4 video inputs, native AI4 neural network architectures, and unprecedented training compute resources to deliver a smoother, safer, and more efficient driving experience. Whether navigating city streets or cruising highways, Godfrey adapts seamlessly to real-world conditions, providing dynamic routing around closures, improved collision avoidance, and more intuitive speed profiles.

What’s New in Godfrey:

  • Full-resolution AI4 video inputs for clearer understanding of surroundings
  • Native AI4 neural network architectures enabling advanced perception and control
  • 4.2x data scaling to train on vastly more real-world scenarios
  • 5x training compute scaling with the Cortex cluster for faster learning
  • Reduced photon-to-control latency by 2x for quicker responses to changes
  • Speed profiles on both city streets and highways for smoother acceleration and braking
  • Improved reward predictions for collision avoidance ensuring safer maneuvers
  • Dynamic routing around road closures displayed when detected by the fleet

Additional Improvements Now Integrated (Including Cybertruck-Specific Enhancements):

  • 3x model size scaling and 3x model context length scaling for richer understanding
  • Integrated unpark, reverse, and park capabilities for complex maneuvers
  • Audio inputs to better handle emergency vehicles
  • Improved reward predictions for navigation enhancing route choices
  • Reduced false braking and slower driving in parking lots for gentler behavior
  • Redesigned controller for smoother, more accurate tracking
  • Support for destination options including pulling over, parking in a spot, driveway, or garage
  • Efficient representation of maps and navigation inputs for quicker decision-making
  • Improved camera cleaning to handle camera occlusions for reliable vision

Editorial Note: This version of FSD, particularly for the Cybertruck, integrates all these improvements, delivering a next-level autonomous driving experience that sets a new standard for Tesla vehicles.
